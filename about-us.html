ABOUT US

Bulatovic & Partners

The law firm Bulatović & partners (hereinafter “the Law Firm”, or “we”) was established in 2010. Today the law firm numbers 4 attorneys, 1 junior attorney, and support staff. The office headquarters are located on 24 Bulevar kralja Aleksandra, Belgrade. The partners attended the Law Faculty in Belgrade and graduated between years 2000 and 2007 and have since then gained extensive practice by leading in-house legal departments of larger companies, working for the companies abroad, representing clients in various courts, and practicing as attorneys.

The Law Firm is qualified as medium Serbian law firm in terms of the number of fully qualified attorneys specializing in commercial and corporate law and providing services solely (with very rare exceptions) to legal entities (companies, governments, financial institutions). We are able to provide a broad range of legal services to a great variety of clients. Among our clients are the biggest Serbian companies such as several domestic and foreign investment and commercial banks, foreign and domestic leasing companies, industrial corporations, domestic and foreign pharmaceutical companies and select individuals.

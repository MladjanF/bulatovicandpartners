AREAS OF PRACTICE

Areas of Practice

The core of our business are the following practice areas: banking, general corporate practice, real estate transactions, competition law, labour law, construction, public procurement, insolvency law, litigation and dispute resolution.

The firm assists clients active in various economic sectors/industries such as banking and finance, pharmaceutical, construction, media, health care and retail.

Our experience and credentials regarding some of our key areas of expertise are described below.


M&A, CAPITAL MARKETS, BANKING

We have developed a nationally recognized expertise in the full range of merger and acquisition transactions. The firm acts for purchasers, sellers and financial advisors in stock and asset deals, mergers, takeovers, restructuring and joint ventures, both Serbian and cross-border. We have extensive experience in competition issues related to M&A and approval processes.


CORPORATE AND COMMERCIAL LAW

The firm provides legal advice to private and public companies, domestic and international financial institutions in relation to general corporate and commercial matters, including the incorporation and management of companies and joint ventures. We advise our clients on various general business law matters, including counselling on a range of general securities law issues and the organisation and governance of corporations. We can aid our clients in structuring and forming new companies, partnerships, contractual and institutional joint ventures, limited liability companies and other entities.

The experts in the firm have gained a relevant experience in merger, demerger and acquisition transactions, take-over bids as well as restructuring and reorganisation of companies, also by means of share transfer, or sale of going concerns. The firm also offers assistance in relation to negotiation and drafting of all relevant documentation, including shareholders' agreements and stock options, with an approach consisting in the analysis of legal issues as part of a larger context that includes strategic and commercial aspects. The law firm gives special attention to the application of specific provisions of contract law at drafting of contracts between commercial companies.


REAL ESTATE TRANSACTIONS

Our team is providing legal support in Real Estate Transactions. Our real estate practice encompasses initial planning, acquisition, financing, investment, development, construction, management and sales. The lawyers advise clients in all phases of the real estate projects, from providing counsel in due diligence process, contract negotiations during the acquisition phase and resolving technical issues, to assisting the clients with management during the operations phase and lease and sell of the property. We assist developers in the acquisition, development, funding and leasing of commercial, mixed-use and residential properties and developments. Furthermore, we advise our clients on long term lease agreements and other real estate matters.


EMPLOYMENT LAW

Our labour law team composed of seasoned and highly experienced attorneys offers:

- advice on drafting of the employment contract, internal acts, rules and collective agreements;
- advice on individual employment relationships (contract conclusion, contract performance, termination of employment contracts);
- representation in individual and collective employment disputes in front of the competent courts;
- advice on the appointment, dismissal and employment contract termination of the management;
- advice on the worker redundancy and mass lay-offs procedures;

LITIGATION

We are regularly involved in litigation in commercial disputes arising from various business transactions on behalf of domestic and foreign, corporate clients.

Our team of experienced litigators regularly represents clients in important disputes (with value in excess of EUR 10 million) and other high profile cases.


REGULATORY ISSUES AND PRODUCT LIABILITY

We advise on many regulatory issues and regularly represent clients before different regulatory authorities.

A group of highly qualified attorney assists clients with a great variety of data protection issues and represents clients in front of our Data Protection Authority.

We also assist our clients regarding product liability issues and product recalls on regular basis.


HEALTHCARE

Our healthcare practice has over the years of advising many of the major multinational pharmaceutical company developed extensive experience. We offer to our clients on daily basis practical and proactive advices among others on:

- pricing and reimbursement regulations and proceedings;
- promotional materials compliance;
- clinical trials;
- government and health insurance relations;
- IP rights infringement litigation;
- wholesale distributorships ;
